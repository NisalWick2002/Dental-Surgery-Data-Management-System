About us

Pelawatta Dental Surgery offers a range of family dental services from our convenient location in Pelawatta, Battaramulla.

Our dentists, Dr. D.M Abeyrathne and Dr. T. Perera, warmly welcome new patients of all ages. Whether you’re seeking a gentle, friendly dentist to provide routine checkups or are hoping to achieve the smile you’ve always wanted, or require something in between, we’re here to exceed your expectations.

Why Choose Pelawatta Dental Surgery?

There may be other dentists in our area, but we’d like to think that there are some key benefits that set us apart:


  • Same-day appointments for immediate attention

  • Saturday & Sunday appointments for those with busy schedules

  • Late opening hours

  • Emergency care 24 hours a day for patients of record
